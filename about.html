Our Approach

Equine-assisted therapy at Equiatude.

Our team specializes in equine-assisted therapy programs designed to address emotional, physical, and behavioral challenges. Every session is tailored to the client's comfort and needs — and we regularly see breakthroughs in clients who haven't responded to traditional talk therapy.

Grooming

Brushing, cleaning hooves, and tacking up build trust through quiet, hands-on connection.

Riding Lessons

Exercises focused on relaxation, confidence, and trust — paced to each client's comfort.

Liberty Training

Guiding a horse using only energy and body language, on foot — building deep self-awareness.

The Benefits

What equestrian work can improve.

PhysicalBalance, strength, coordination
EmotionalFocus, self-awareness, empathy
SocialConfidence, independence, connection
