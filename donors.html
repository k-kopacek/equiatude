Here we want to thank all of our Donors and Volunteers for making our Mission a reality.

We are constantly working to build a new and improved, safe environment to share with our community. You have been a blessing to us and our horses who truly help women in need!!

The Round Pen Arena is so much more then what it seems. The round pen allows us to not only work and train our horses outside of their therapy duties but is also for the women taking lessons. The enclosed arena helps the horse and client feel safe. The client and horse can truly bond without a rope or halter known as working at Liberty, moving the horse using only energy, body language and position. This requires the client to dig deep finding their inner calmness and awareness. Clients end the session feeling more connected and confident than when they went in because the horse is CHOOSING to follow their instruction without force. Donors and Volunteers Thank you for our Round Pen!!
