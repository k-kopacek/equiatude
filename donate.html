Focused on providing full transparency, we are committed to keeping you informed about our expenses and how your donations contribute to the well-being of our equine companions. To reduce costs, we handle all labor ourselves. Stay up to date on the projects your donations support by visiting our Facebook page.

Your generous donations play a crucial role in supporting our mission by funding essential aspects of our operations, including:

  • Projects to maintain safe and clean facilities for both horses and clients
  • Providing safe and appropriate equipment for therapy sessions
  • Nutritional support for our equine partners, including feed, hay, and necessary supplements
  • Maintenance of hoof health and veterinary care
  • Ensuring the overall safety and well-being of our clients

Thank you for standing with us in our commitment to serve survivors of abuse through the healing power of equine therapy. Together, we can make a profound difference in the lives of those who need it most.
