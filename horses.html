Meet Our Unique Horses

Teddy

Teddy came to us as a rescue from the Santa Anita track, extremely apprehensive about humans and injured. With consistent rehabilitation we are able to teach him that working with humans can be fun and rewarding, not scary. Teddy has been with us for over 8 years. He is a happy boy and will always need special attention which we are honored to give!

Callie

Callie is a gorgeous Off-Track Thoroughbred who has transitioned into the official "head therapist" under saddle for our non-profit program. Through dedicated training and plenty of love, Callie has found true relaxation under saddle and beautifully channels her sensitive nature into helping her riders. She has an incredible knack for tuning into people, helping them find their own calm and confidence just as she found hers. Callie proves every day that there is beautiful life after the track.
