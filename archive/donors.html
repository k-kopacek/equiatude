The Round Pen: more than an arena.

The Round Pen is where therapy and training meet. Inside its enclosed space, clients work horses at liberty — without a rope or halter — guiding them using only energy, body language, and presence. It's a vulnerable, powerful exercise: the horse chooses to follow, and that choice builds a client's confidence like nothing else.

Thank you, donors and volunteers, for making this space possible.
