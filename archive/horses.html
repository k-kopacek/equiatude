Off the Track, Onto the Farm

Meet our horses.

Every horse at Equiatude is a retired racehorse given time, patience, and purpose. Here are two of our partners in healing.

Teddy

Rescued from Santa Anita

Teddy came to us as a rescue from the Santa Anita track — injured and extremely apprehensive about humans. After 9 months of patient rehabilitation, he learned that working with people could be fun and rewarding, not scary. Teddy has been ours for over three years. He's a happy boy who will always need a little extra attention, which we're honored to give.

Callie

Finding Balance Again

Callie is a retired racehorse working on stretching long and low into the contact while finding relaxation and balance. At first she was tense and co-dependent — confidence now prevails. It's a joy to watch her move comfortably and trust herself again.

Every horse here has a story like theirs.

Your support helps us rescue, rehabilitate, and give purpose to more off-track thoroughbreds.
